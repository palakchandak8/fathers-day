Happy Father's
Day, Papa 🎈

From your biggest fan, your toughest critic, and the one person you'll always be the hero to.

Dear Papa,

I don't say this enough, so I'm putting it somewhere you can come back to: you are my hero. Not the kind from movies with capes and grand entrances, but the quieter, sturdier kind — the one who was simply always there, every single time it mattered.

You stood between me and a world that can be loud and unkind, and you made sure it never reached me before I was ready for it. But you never once boxed me in. You let me wander, question, try, fail, and try again — you protected my heart while setting my path free. That balance is rare, and I don't think I've ever thanked you for how hard that must have been to get right.

Every option I've had, every door that's been open to me — somewhere behind it, quietly, is you. Holding it open and stepping back so I could walk through on my own.

So today, of all days, I just want you to feel how loved and how seen you are. Thank you for being my safe place and my launching pad, all at once.

I love you, Papa. Always your girl.

— Palak 💌
